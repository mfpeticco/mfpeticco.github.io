Activities

These are all the activities and clubs I have participated in!

Swimming and Diving Team

I've been on the Newburgh Sharks club team since 5th grade, when I was 8 years old, and on my school's varsity team since 8th grade. Now, I have amassed numerous varsity sectional's cuts requiring specific time standards for different events.

NFA Robotics

In 9th grade, I was a part of the NFA/Excelsior robotics team for the first time. A robot was designed that was supposed to be able to lift up small 3" diameters balls for a First Tech Challenge (FTC) Competition. The next year, I became the co-facilitator of the team with one of my friends, and we designed a bot that could scoop up balls and throw them several feet. Now, in my senior year I will be back on the team with a new project: to create a combat robot. Read more about it on my robotics page.

Academic Societies

Ever since 7th grade, I have been a part of multiple academic societies such. These include the National Junior Honor Society, New York State Science Honor Society, and National Honor Society. All of these societies have strict guidelines and rules for entry whether it be an essay, certain grade standards, classes, and more.
