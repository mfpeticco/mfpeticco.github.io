High School Academics

I was in a high school program known as Excelsior Academy, a Pathways In Technology Early College Highschool (PTECH) program that is in partnership with IBM. This program allows students to attain both a high school diploma and an Associate's Degree in Applied Science for either Networking or Cyber Security from SUNY Orange. These are real college classes taken on an actual college campus (SUNY Orange Middletown Campus) with college professors alongside other SUNY students. Although these courses are taken at the expense of being unable to take many AP classes, I am happy with the challenging and fascinating coursework I have been presented with. This has allowed me to get on the Dean's list.

Here is a sample of the coursework I've completed:

  • CIT 105 - Data Comm. & Networking
  • CSS 226 - Cyber Crime Investigations
  • CRJ 101 - Criminal Justice
  • CIT 217 - Linux/Unix
  • CIT 118 - Operating Systems
  • CIT 116 - Networking 1&2
