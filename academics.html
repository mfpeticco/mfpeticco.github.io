High School Academics

I was in a high school program known as Newburgh PTECH (Formerly Excelsior Academy), a Pathways In Technology Early College Highschool (PTECH) program that was formed partnership with Newburgh Free Academy, IBM, and SUNY Orange. This program allows students to attain both a high school diploma and an Associate's Degree in Applied Science for either Networking or Cyber Security from SUNY Orange. These are real college classes taken on an actual college campus (SUNY Orange Middletown Campus) with college professors alongside other SUNY students. Although these courses are taken at the expense of being unable to take many AP classes, I am very content with the challenging and fascinating coursework I was given the opportunity to take.

Here is a sample of the coursework I've completed:

  • CIT 105 - Data Communications & Networking
  • CSS 226 - Cyber Crime Investigations
  • CRJ 101 - Criminal Justice
  • CIT 117 - Linux/Unix
  • CIT 118 - Operating Systems
  • CIT 116 - Networking 1
  • CIT 203 - Networking 2
